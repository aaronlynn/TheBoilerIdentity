The Boiler Identity

Leaderboard

The Boiler Identity is our version of the popular board game "Spyfall". In each round, 3-8 players are given a location and a role. However, one player is a spy, and doesn't know the current location. Players have eight minutes to ask each other questions about where they are in an attempt to catch the spy. The spy also has eight minutes to guess the location. When the eight minutes are up, each player gets an attempt to accuse another player of being the spy. You better have a convincing argument though, because the spy must be voted on unanimously. Good luck!
